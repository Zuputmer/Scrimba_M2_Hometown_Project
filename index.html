Visit Coimbatore!

Immerse yourself in the tranquility of the hills while visiting this city known as the Manchester of South India!

Things to see and do in Coimbatore

Go on a trek

Being nestled by the south indian mountain ranges, Coimbatore provides you the perfect opportunity to unwind and experience nature in it's purest form. With frequent wildlife sightings, going on a trek in one of the many nearby trails is sure to keep you exhilarated!

Visit our temples

With hundreds of temples dotting the landscape and known for being a city of temples, Coimbatore is home to one of the oldest temples in India, built in the 2nd century. Being a living testament to the vibrant ancient history of the land, a visit to our temples is sure to leave you in awe of ancient architecture and design!

Enjoy the local cuisine

Served on a banana leaf, the traditional south indian meal is a must-try! With the perfect blend of spices, the cuisine of Coimbatore is unique experience that cannot be described in words!
